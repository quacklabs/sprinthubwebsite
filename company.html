Our Story

    Back in 2014 when the smart phone market in Africa became mainstream our Founder started his Android app development journey as a final year computer science student. He later met our Lead Mobile Engineer in 2016, a technophile with over 4 years experience (at the time) in building Blackberry, iOS and Android apps using Objective C, Java and cross platform tool like Xamarin (C#). They quickly formed a tight freelancing partnership and worked with different startups accross Nigeria and the US while saving up funds to build a mobile app team, which they eventually did with Sprinthub Mobile in January 2018.

Today, we are the only mobile-focused software development team in Nigeria. With a leadership team that has over 8 year experience in building mobile apps, it was quite easy to find and groom the best mobile app talents. These days we don't only create great apps, we also help our clients answer strategic questions about what they are trying to build, their target market and in what order they should release features.
